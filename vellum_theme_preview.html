Introduction

Knowledge work demands a quality of focus that is increasingly rare. The ability to concentrate without distraction on a cognitively demanding task is becoming both more valuable and more scarce.

Clarity about what matters provides clarity about what does not.

Key Principles

Work deeply, embrace boredom, quit social media selectively. Use time-blocking to structure your day around meaningful output.
